description=Universal coordinate converter supporting all QGIS CRS (200+ systems) with native BFTM support for Burkina Faso. Convert between any CRS: WGS84, UTM zones 1-60, Adindan, NAD83, ETRS89, GDA2020, and custom PROJ strings. Features: single conversion, batch CSV processing, interactive map picking, support for DD, DMS (with French notations O/E/N/S), and meters.
